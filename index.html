Liam Stedman

Berlin-based photographer, graphic designer, painter and programmer

software and computers

I have used and managed Debian- and Arch-based Linux systems, as well as Windows 7 — 11, on both workstations and servers. Additionally, I automated many previously manual tasks using shell / Python scripting for different small clients.
I have worked with library-specific technology and the ILS Koha while setting up and maintaining a small school library.
I do most of my typesetting in Typst, an up-and-coming replacement to LaTeX. Because Typst is still very much in active development, I also use LaTeX or Adobe InDesign for some projects. Additionally, I am experienced with web technologies and web design.
I am currently teaching myself Rust by writing a proof assistant for propositional logic, which combines my interests in computer science, philosophy, and mathematics.

film and vfx

I have written, filmed, and edited music videos, image films and short student film projects. I am experienced with Blender and Adobe After Effects for VFX work.
